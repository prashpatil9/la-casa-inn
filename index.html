About the Inn

A Pasadena Original

Built in 1961 as a Hyatt Chalet — with its distinctive A-frame roof and diamond windows — La Casa Inn has welcomed guests to Pasadena for over six decades. Our family acquired the property in 2010 and has spent years thoughtfully renovating every room while preserving the character that makes this place unlike anything else in the city.
